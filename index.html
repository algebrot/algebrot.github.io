Cora Coleman

I am a fourth year CSE PhD student, advised by William Griswold at the University of California, San Diego. My research interests include Software Engineering and Human-Computer Interaction. I passed the PhD Research Exam and obtained an MS in Computer Science from the University of California, San Diego in 2020. I graduated with a BA in Computer Science from New College of Florida in 2018.


Research Projects

Cloud Tools and Development Tasks
Survey and observational user study of cloud developers to understand tool preferences as they vary across developement tasks. Began as a part of a summer internship project with the IBM Hybrid Cloud research group. With Nick Mitchell and William Griswold.

Invariants Plugin
IntelliJ plugin that displays invariant properties and their changes at the method level. I am developing a user study to evaluate the usefulness of invariants in programming. With Massimiliano Menarini and William Griswold.
