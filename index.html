2019-2020

During this quarter I will be the TA for CSE 210 Software Engineering, taught by Dr. William Griswold. This graduate-level course takes students through a quarter-long software project with a real client using Agile developement methodology. Each week research papers are read and discussed, along with chapters from Head First Software Development and The Mythical Man Month.

I was a student volunteer at the 2019 ESRI User Conference in San Diego, California. The group of us student volunteers were very diverse in our disciplindes, ranging from Geography to Biology to History. Research Projects

Invariants Plugin, Winter 2019-Present
IntelliJ plugin that displays invariant properties and their changes at the method level. I am developing a user study to evaluate the usefulness of invariants in debugging. With Massimiliano Menarini, led by William Griswold.

Synthesis of Layout Constraints, Fall 2018-Present
An interactive constraint-based layout tool for synthesizing generalizable layouts from few examples. I am developing the UI for our tool as part of a case study. With Dylan Lukes and John Sarracino, led by Nadia Polikarpova and Sorin Lerner.
