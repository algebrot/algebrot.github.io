Cora Coleman

Computer Science PhD student at UCSD advised by Bill Griswold. My research interests include Software Engineering and Human-Computer Interaction. Member of the Design Lab and Programming Systems Group at UCSD. I passed the PhD Research Exam and obtained an MS in Computer Science from UCSD in 2020. I graduated with a BA in Computer Science from New College of Florida in 2018.


Research Projects

Cloud Tools and Development Tasks
Conducted a survey and observational user study of cloud developers to understand tool preferences as they vary across development tasks. With Nick Mitchell and Bill Griswold.

Invariants Plugin
IntelliJ plugin that displays invariant properties and their changes at the method level. I am developing a user study to evaluate the usefulness of invariants in programming. With Massimiliano Menarini and Bill Griswold.
