Cora Coleman

I am a third year CSE PhD student, advised by William Griswold at the University of California, San Diego. My research interests include Software Development, Human-Computer Interaction, and GIS. I passed the PhD Research Exam and obtained an MS from the University of California, San Diego in 2020. I graduated with a BA in Computer Science from New College of Florida in 2018.


Research Projects

Invariants Plugin, Winter 2019-Present
IntelliJ plugin that displays invariant properties and their changes at the method level. I am developing a user study to evaluate the usefulness of invariants in debugging. With Massimiliano Menarini, led by William Griswold.

Synthesis of Layout Constraints, Fall 2018-Present
An interactive constraint-based layout tool for synthesizing generalizable layouts from few examples. I am developing the UI for our tool as part of a user study. With Dylan Lukes and John Sarracino, led by Nadia Polikarpova and Sorin Lerner.
